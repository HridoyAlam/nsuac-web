About Us

What we are

North South University Athletics Club (NSUAC) is the ultimate sports club of North South University. It was established in 1995 and has been offering sporting and competitive activity for the last 25 years. The club takes immense pride in representing NSU at many extramural sports events in different categories and brought glory and honor to the Institute. NSUAC is one of the oldest and prestigious clubs of North South University.

The purpose of NSUAC is to bring out the best of the athletes in NSU, prepare, and groom them for extramural competition. It offers an opportunity to students who desire to participate and develop their skills in particular sports. Students are able to derive pleasure and add spirit to their monotonous academic life as NSUAC provides a chance to the participants to engage in many intramural sports events. NSUAC has also represented NSU at many Inter-University sports competitions and came out as champions in Cricket, Football, Basketball, Table Tennis, Badminton and many more.

The principal events organized by NSUAC has helped in emanating the best of sporting talents among the students of NSU.
