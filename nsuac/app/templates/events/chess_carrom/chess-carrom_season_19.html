Assalamu Alaikum everyone. For the first Time in NSU history NSU Athletics Club presents you Intra NSU Chess Master & Carrom Champ 2019. The event consists of two segments, i.e, Chess tournament and Carrom tournament.
Both the tournaments will be played following the Round Robbin System. In the Chess tournament players will participate individually. In the Carrom tournament players need to make a team of two and then participate. Both the tournaments are mixed gender tournaments, i.e, open for both male and female.
Players can participate in any one segment of the event. To participate in the event players need to register themselves first and the registration will be a first come first serve process.
# Registration is on 12th June, 2019 & 13th June, 2019 (10:30 AM - 05:30 PM) at the Plaza area.
#You will have to bring a photo copy of your NSU ID.
# Event will start from 14th June, 2019 at the NSU Indoor. The participants list, Fixtures and game rules will be provided on 14th June, 2019 at 09:00 PM in this event page.
