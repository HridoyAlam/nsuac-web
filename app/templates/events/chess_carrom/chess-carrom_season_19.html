Assalamu Alaikum everyone. For the first Time in NSU history NSU Athletics Club presents you Intra NSU Chess Master & Carrom Champ 2019. The event consists of two segments, i.e, Chess tournament and Carrom tournament.
Both the tournaments will be played following the Round Robbin System. In the Chess tournament players will participate individually. In the Carrom tournament players need to make a team of two and then participate. Both the tournaments are mixed gender tournaments, i.e, open for both male and female.
Players can participate in any one segment of the event. To participate in the event players need to register themselves first and the registration will be a first come first serve process.
# Registration is on 12th June, 2019 & 13th June, 2019 (10:30 AM - 05:30 PM) at the Plaza area.
#You will have to bring a photo copy of your NSU ID.
# Event will start from 14th June, 2019 at the NSU Indoor. The participants list, Fixtures and game rules will be provided on 14th June, 2019 at 09:00 PM in this event page.

Everyone likes to get recognition for their hard work. There are reasons to have these glamorous prizes, after all. Receiving rewards at the end of the day for the hard work we put in is sometimes the best way to boost someone’s energy. To keep in mind, on June 18th, 2019, our Intra NSU Chess Master and Carrom Champ 2019 event came to a successful conclusion. The chief guest at our awards ceremony was Rani Hamid, one of the pioneers of the sports industry and the first female international chess master of our country. Rani Hamid was accompanied by our University Professor Gias U Ahsan, Former Pro-Vice-Chancellor and Faculty Adviser of our Club, Senior Lecturer, Md. Mehedi Hasan Sir and Former Director of Student Affairs Office, Senior Lecturer, Mrs. Parisha Shakur Madam. Tamjid Ahmed Tanu was the finalist of Intra NSU Chess Master 2019, and Imran Mahmud Sadman was the Runner up. They managed to retain the title among 64 competitors, and the champion team of Intra NSU Carrom Champ 2019 was Team Dictator, and the runners-up team was Titan. They also compete against 32 other teams for the championship. With all this, Intra NSU Chess Master and Carrom Champ 2019 has come to a great and successful outcome.
