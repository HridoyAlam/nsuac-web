NSU Athletics Club is here with the 2nd edition of NORTH SOUTH UNIVERSITY CRICKET LEAGUE (NCL). This is a cricket event which lured a number of students and even the faculty and admin personnel. As before, it will be an event where faculties as well as students will participate in a two phase tournament. A tournament will be held for all the clubs of NSU and a seperate tournament will be held for the faculties & admin personnel as well. The venue of the event is NSU Field.
Participating Clubs: Art & Photography Club, Athletics Club, Bioscience Club, Cine & Drama Club, Communication Club, Computer & Engineering Club, Debate Club, Diversity Club, Earth Club, Ethics Club, Finance Club, Human Resource Club, Law & Mooting Society, Master of Business Administration Club, Model UN Club, Pharmacy Club, Shangskritik Shangathan, Social Service Club, Young Economists’ Forum, Young Entrepreneurs Society,
The tournament will kick start on March 6, and end on March 17, 2018. So, come up and support your favourite club and faculties in a event that will mesmerize and blow everyone's mind.
